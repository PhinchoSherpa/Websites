Sahara

Poem from the perspective of Leslie Cohen

I'm a lesbian women who is proud to be who I am.
I wondered if people could accept me.
I hear my people's excitement in Sahara.
I see only love.
I want freedom of expression.
I am a proud lesbian.

I understand love and not hate.
I say love is genderless.
I dream of having our world like Sahara.
I worry for people's lives.
I stand for lesbianism and feminism.
I hope to be a change.
I am proud.
